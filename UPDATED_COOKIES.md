## üìù How to Update on Render

1. Go to https://dashboard.render.com/
2. Select your "social-fetch-pro" service
3. Go to **Environment** tab
4. For each variable above:
   - Find the existing variable (e.g., `TWITTER_COOKIE`)
   - Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon)
   - Replace the old value with the new value from above
   - Click **Save**
5. After updating all variables, click **Save Changes** at the bottom
6. Wait 2-3 minutes for Render to redeploy
